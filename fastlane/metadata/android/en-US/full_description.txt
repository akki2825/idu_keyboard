Official keyboard for the Idu Mishmi language. Based on HeliBoard, a privacy-conscious open-source keyboard derived from AOSP / OpenBoard.
Features full support for Idu Mishmi with custom accent markers and diacritics. Does not use internet permission, and thus is 100% offline.

🌟 **Idu Mishmi Language Support:**
• Complete support for Idu Mishmi characters and diacritics
• Special long-press popup keys for vowels with Idu Mishmi accent markers
• Includes characters: ə, ə̃, ə˞, ə̃˞, Ə, Ə̃, Ə˞, Ə̃˞ and more
• Optimized for Idu Mishmi language typing experience

📱 **Long Press Guide - Quick Access to Special Characters:**

**Vowels with Idu Mishmi Characters:**
• **Long press 'e'** → è, é, ê, ë, ē, ẽ, ə, ə̃, ə̀, ə́, ə̄, ə˞, ə̃˞, ə̀˞, ə́˞, ə̄˞
• **Long press 'E'** → È, É, Ê, Ë, Ē, Ẽ, Ə, Ə̃, Ə̀, Ə́, Ə̄, Ə˞, Ə̃˞, Ə̀˞, Ə́˞, Ə̄˞
• **Long press 'a'** → à, á, â, ä, ã, ā (2-column layout)
• **Long press 'A'** → À, Á, Â, Ä, Ã, Ā (2-column layout)
• **Long press 'i'** → ì, í, î, ï, ī, ĩ
• **Long press 'I'** → Ì, Í, Î, Ï, Ī, Ĩ
• **Long press 'o'** → ò, ó, ö, ō, õ
• **Long press 'O'** → Ò, Ó, Ö, Ō, Õ
• **Long press 'u'** → ù, ú, ü, ū, ũ
• **Long press 'U'** → Ù, Ú, Ü, Ū, Ũ

**Special Features:**
• **Two-column layout** for 'a' and 'A' keys for better organization
• **Idu Mishmi specific characters** (ə, Ə with various diacritics)
• **Standard English accents** alongside Idu Mishmi characters
• **Easy access** to all special characters without switching layouts
